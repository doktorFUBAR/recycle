About us

At Re-Cycle we offer eco-friendly guided cycling tours around beautiful Trondheim.

Trondheim is one of Norway’s leading cities when it comes to enviromental care. The city is surrounded by beautiful fjords and mountains.

Exploring by bicycle helps keeping the surroundings and you healthy.

Values

We live in a time where taking care of nature is more important than ever. All our bicyles are made from recycled materials right in our shop in Trondheim.

In the digital age we also focus on keeping people healthy, and we offer our customers a green lunch on our longer tours.

F.A.Q.

Q: Can I bring my kids on the tours?

A: You can bring your children on all the tours. However children under the age of 12 are only reccomended on our shorter tour «Midtbyen».

Q: Are the tours listed on your website the only options?

A: The tours we offer rotate based on seasons. However, we do customize tours. Contact us with your wishes, and we can design the tour of your dreams.

Q: Can I use my own bike on a tour?

A: Yes! We also offer to do a check-up on your bicycle and re-fill your tires.
